Cultural Representation Note

These 3D models are based on extensive historical and cultural research, primarily focusing on Ifugao heritage and extending to other Indigenous traditions of the Philippines. Please note that these visualizations are digital recreations subject to the constraints of real-time rendering. Minor variations in texture or material appearance may occur compared to physical historical artifacts. We are committed to honoring the cultural significance of these items and welcome feedback to ensure our portrayals remain respectful and accurate.
