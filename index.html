Araro Games

Araro Games is a passionate group of students from FEU Institute of Technology pursuing a degree in Information Technology with a specialization in Animation and Game Development. United by their creativity and love for Filipino culture, the team aims to create interactive games that both entertain and educate players.


Through their projects, Araro Games seeks to remind the youth of today about the importance of preserving Filipino heritage, traditions, and cultural values in a rapidly modernizing world. By combining storytelling, visual art, animation, and gameplay mechanics, the group develops experiences that showcase elements of Philippine history, customs, and rural life in engaging and relatable ways.

Academia De Covina

Academia De Covina is a private educational institution in Imus Cavite where they take pride in teaching its students how to navigate life while following the teachings of the word of God.

KulturaQuest

KulturaQuest is a 3D interactive adventure that brings the rich heritage of the Philippines to life. Players explore traditional environments, solve culturally inspired puzzles utilizing ancient scripts like Baybayin, and uncover the history behind artifacts such as the lingling-o charms and Kalsa gongs.
